Our Assessment Framework

1. Environment Discovery

We gain read-only access to your cloud environment to enumerate assets, users, roles, and configurations.

2. Automated Compliance Checks

We use automated tools to check your configuration against CIS benchmarks and other industry best practices.

3. Manual In-Depth Review

Our cloud security experts perform a manual review to identify nuanced issues and business logic flaws.

4. Contextual Risk Reporting

You receive a report that not only lists findings but also explains the specific risk to your business and how to fix it.
