Prioritized Findings

Configuration Reviews

Our Process

1. Asset Discovery

We map your network to identify all active devices, open ports, and running services to define the assessment scope.

2. Vulnerability Scanning

Using a suite of commercial and open-source tools, we scan for known vulnerabilities, missing patches, and weak configurations.

3. Manual Validation

Our experts manually validate automated findings to eliminate false positives and assess the true risk of each vulnerability.

4. Strategic Reporting

We provide a clear, actionable report detailing our findings, their potential impact, and prioritized recommendations for remediation.
